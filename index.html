Coffee Cupcake with Whipped Cream

The perfect little cake to go along with your daily coffee. It’s very easy to make and turns out wonderful — it will surely impress. Try it and let me know what you think!
Time: 1h10
Yield: 12 servings
Difficulty: easy

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups of warm water
  • 1 tablespoon instant coffee
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of oil
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 2/3 cup of cocoa powder
  • 2 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 2 cups of ready-made whipped cream
  • Coffee beans for decoration
  • 1 can of sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon instant coffee
  • 1 tablespoon cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons butter

How to Prepare

In a blender, blend together the water, instant coffee, eggs, oil, sugar, and cocoa powder until smooth. Transfer to a bowl, add the flour and baking powder, and mix with a spoon. Pour the batter into cupcake liners placed in a muffin tin, arranging them side by side. Bake in a preheated medium oven for about 25 minutes, or until baked through and golden. Remove and let cool.

For the filling, cook all the ingredients over medium heat, stirring well until thickened. Turn off the heat, let cool, and transfer to a piping bag with a round tip. Set aside. Place the whipped cream into another piping bag fitted with a star tip and set aside.

To assemble, make a hole in the center of each cupcake and fill with the reserved coffee brigadeiro. Decorate the tops with the whipped cream, piping in circles. Garnish with coffee beans and refrigerate until ready to serve.
